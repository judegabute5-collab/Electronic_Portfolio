SCHOOL EVENTS

Reflection

BUWAN NG WIKA

During the Buwan ng Wika celebration, I learned how important it is to appreciate and use our own language. It reminded me that Filipino is more than just a subject—it represents who we are as a nation. I can apply what I learned by using Filipino in daily communication and respecting our different cultures. I joined our class declamation performance as part of the event. Buwan ng Wika celebrates our national language and its significance in building pride and unity. This event helps strengthen our cultural identity and encourages everyone to value the beauty of our language.

SCIENCE MONTH

I learned that science plays a big role in solving real-world problems and improving our quality of life. I can apply it by being curious, asking questions, and using scientific thinking in daily decisions. I participated by joining the quiz bee, poster making, and research colloquium. Science Month encourages innovation and shows how science connects to everyday life. It is important because it promotes awareness of scientific knowledge and inspires students to explore the world through discovery.

INTRAMURALS

I learned the value of teamwork, sportsmanship, and discipline through friendly competition. I can apply it by working well with others, being respectful, and staying active. I joined volleyball and sack race—our team placed 2nd and 1st respectively. Intramurals is not just about winning but about unity, hard work, and self-improvement. It promotes friendship and overall well-being in the school community.

TEACHERS’ DAY

Teachers’ Day made me appreciate the hard work and sacrifices that teachers make every day. I can show gratitude by being respectful and doing my best in class. I joined our classroom celebration and gave letters and gifts to express my appreciation. This day honors teachers who shape our future and reminds us that even simple acts of kindness mean a lot. It’s an important celebration that highlights their role in our lives.

AP MONTH

AP Month helped me appreciate our country’s history and the importance of learning about society. I can apply it by being aware of current issues and understanding my role as a student in nation-building. I joined classroom discussions and interactive activities. This event is important because it promotes awareness and appreciation of our culture, heritage, and responsibility as Filipino citizens.
